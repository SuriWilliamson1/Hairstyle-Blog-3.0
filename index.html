  1. Brush your hair to remove tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure with an elastic.
  3. Twist the ponytail loosely and wrap it around the base to form a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few strands for a relaxed, messy look.

ðŸŒ¸ Classic French Braid

  1. Start with brushed, detangled hair.
  2. Divide the top section into three equal strands.
  3. Cross the right strand over the middle, then the left over the new middle.
  4. Add hair to each strand as you braid down the back of your head.
  5. Secure with an elastic at the end.

  1. Put your hair into a high ponytail and secure with an elastic.
  2. Add another elastic a few inches down the ponytail.
  3. Gently tug the section to create a "bubble" effect.
  4. Repeat down the length of the ponytail.
